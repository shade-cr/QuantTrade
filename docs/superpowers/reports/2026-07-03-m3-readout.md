# M3 pooled cross-section — first honest readout (B0003)

**Date:** 2026-07-03
**Verdict:** **NO EDGE**
**Universe:** 35 t0-2006 large-caps (survivor-selected); 9 SPDR sector ETFs as control.
**Configs:** `configs/equity_m3_d1.yaml` (stocks, 10 bps), `configs/equity_m3_etf_d1.yaml` (ETFs, 3 bps).
**Pipeline gates applied:** fixed 0.55 headline threshold; `should_stack` = ≥2 models beating baseline in ≥3/4 folds with ≥30 trades each; `min_trades_per_fold` = 30; NaN Sharpes skipped (never zero-filled) in aggregation; PSR/DSR deflated by the familywise trial count.

> Survivor-universe caveat (B0003 caveat 1): the long side is mechanically inflated and the short side deflated in this universe. Only *within-universe* relative claims ("does the ML meta add value over the primary?") are admissible — no absolute-edge claim is claimable here regardless of the numbers below.

The entire readout below must be read through this lens: these runs are **underpowered by the project's own pre-registered floor.**

## 2. Stack-gate outcomes at the fixed 0.55 threshold (NaN folds skipped)

**Zero of the 8 (primary, model) pools clear the `should_stack` competence gate.**

The pipeline's own per-slice `stack_decision` records **`stack=false` in 70/70 stock and 18/18 ETF decisions**, every single one with reason `"competence: only 0/4 models beat baseline in ≥3/4 folds with ≥30 trades each (need ≥2)"`. The gate requires ≥2 models to clear; the evidence shows at most **one** model (rf) ever fires enough trades, and never across enough folds:

- **ema_cross:** only `rf` produces ≥30 trades in any fold. Across 35 assets × 4 folds, `rf` had a ≥30-trade fold beating baseline in 29/35 *eligible asset-folds* — but this is one model in ≤1 fold per asset, so it fails both the "≥2 models" and the "≥3/4 folds" requirements. `xgb`, `lgbm`, `lr` fire **0 trades in 140/140 folds**.
- **momentum_zscore:** no model reaches the 30-trade bar in any fold pool; `rf`/`lr` fire a handful of trades, the rest zero.

At the **fixed 0.55 headline threshold** (pooled, `long_short_split.json`), trade reachability confirms the same starvation:

## 3. PSR / DSR after familywise deflation

**No pool has a computable PSR or DSR — all values are NaN in all 88 per-slice `psr_dsr.json` files.** The assumed trial count for deflation is stated in every file: **`n_trials_familywise = 112`** (per-primary lower bound = 4 models × 4 folds × 7 threshold-grid points = 112).

The DSR is undefined not by accident but by starvation: the deflated-Sharpe variance estimate needs ≥2 sampled trial Sharpes, but across all 88 per-slice files, **58 have `n_trial_sharpes_sampled = 1`** (one usable cell) and **30 have `= 0`** (24 stock momentum_zscore + 6 ETF ema_cross files with zero models firing any trade), so `n_trials_effective_clusters = 1` or undefined ("starved cell — N=familywise conservative fallback"). Where a raw per-trade Sharpe even exists (e.g. ABT `ema_cross/rf`: SR/trade ≈ 0.095 over 190 trades), it **cannot be deflated** and would in any case be crushed by a 112-trial correction. There is no candidate to promote.

## 4. Long/short split — does any edge survive on the SHORT side?

**No.** The only tradeable pool at 0.55 is stocks `ema_cross/rf`:

| Side | n_trades | mean pnl/trade | hit ratio | net Sharpe |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Long | 3,223 | +0.0062 | 0.338 | **+1.58** |
| Short | 197 | **−0.0839** | **0.005** | **−7.39** |

The apparent long-side Sharpe of +1.58 is exactly the survivor-universe long-side inflation B0003 caveat 1 warns about — these 35 names were selected *because* they survived and rose from 2006. The short side, where a genuine skill would have to show up symmetrically, is **catastrophic**: 197 trades, a 0.5% hit ratio, −0.084 mean pnl/trade, Sharpe −7.39. A long-only "edge" that inverts to −7.4 Sharpe on shorts in a survivor universe is a **flag, not a result.** Every other pool has too few (or zero) trades on either side to evaluate.

## 5. ETF control (frozen interpretation rule)

Per the FROZEN rule in the `configs/equity_m3_etf_d1.yaml` header, applied as written: **"ETF run fails min-trades gates → report 'control underpowered'; count neither way."**

The ETF control **fails the min-trades gates**: `stack=false` in 18/18 decisions (all "competence"), 6/8 pools fire 0 trades at 0.55, and the single tradeable pool (`ema_cross/rf`, 131 long trades, Sharpe 0.45, no shorts) is one model on one side. Its own `pooled_effective_N` (718.4 / 685.3) is *also* below the 799 floor. The control is therefore **underpowered and counts neither way** — it can neither corroborate nor refute a stock-side signal, because there is no stock-side signal to test and the control itself lacks power.

## 7. Verdict

# NO EDGE

Every independent line of evidence points the same way, and none can be rescued by threshold tuning (barred by spec anyway):

1. **Underpowered by pre-registration** — all four pools sit below the 799 effective-sample refusal floor (647–718); pooling did not relieve starvation.
2. **Gate** — 0/8 pools clear `should_stack`; the pipeline recorded `stack=false` in all 88 decisions, always for "competence."
3. **Statistics** — PSR/DSR uncomputable (NaN) everywhere; the familywise deflation count that *would* apply is 112 trials.
4. **Symmetry** — the sole tradeable pool (`ema_cross/rf`) shows a survivor-inflated +1.58 long Sharpe but a −7.39 short Sharpe: no genuine, side-symmetric skill.
5. **Discrimination** — OOF AUC ~0.5 and calibration collapse (`pct_signals_kept < 5%` in ~100% of folds); nanmedian fold Sharpe is 0.000 for every pool.
6. **Control** — the ETF control is underpowered (fails min-trades gates, effective_N also < floor) and counts neither way per the frozen rule.

This is an **honest null**, not a defect. It is fully consistent with the B0003 hypothesis that daily state-based primaries on this universe are too information-poor (post-uniqueness) for the ML meta to add value; the natural follow-up is richer, lower-autocorrelation features / cross-sectional signals (candidate B0006) rather than any promotion. Whether B0003 closes as an honest no-edge or spawns follow-ups is a human decision to be made reading this document — no auto-promotion.
