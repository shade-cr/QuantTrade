## 2. A/B outcome — v2 changes the fit, not the skill

- Per-asset OOF metrics differ on 34/35 assets (the weights really did change the fit).
- **Panel mean ROC-AUC: 0.5405 (rho1) vs 0.5403 (v2)** — statistically nothing.
- Median sharpe_net per asset: 0.0 in both runs, every asset.
- Trades at threshold 0.55: rho1 ≈ 6 total; v2 ≈ 65 total (calibrated probabilities sit
  slightly higher under the larger effective training mass), but the long/short split shows
  they are noise: rf-long 6 trades, hit ratio 17%, mean pnl −0.59%/trade. No pool/model
  cell has a positive net side.

**Conclusion:** the ρ=1 starvation was real and is fixed (2.6× more usable training mass),
but releasing it does not unlock edge — the cusum_filter primary on this panel carries no
signal for the meta to amplify. This cleanly separates the two hypotheses that were
confounded before B0012: *"the fit is starved"* (true, now resolved) vs *"the primary is
uninformative"* (still true, now isolated as the binding constraint).

## 3. The ENB surprise — structural fork softened

The spec anticipated an ENB ceiling of ~2–3 (assuming panel ρ̄≈0.4), which would have meant
35 correlated large-caps can never behave like more than ~3 independent bets. The measured
PIT panel mean correlation on the last refresh window is **0.18**, giving **ENB ≈ 26**.

Two readings, both honest:

1. The panel has far more contemporaneous diversification than feared — widening the
   universe / pooling is NOT structurally doomed by correlation at D1.
2. ENB is a *ceiling diagnostic on the last window only* and the inference effective-N
   (654) remains the binding number for the floor (799). The gap between 654 and what ENB
   suggests is possible reflects the deliberately conservative ρ=1 inference rule — changing
   that rule is out of scope by design (the firewall) and would need its own methodology
   consult before anyone touches it.

Practical implication: the shortfall to the floor (654 vs 799) is small and the panel is
less redundant than assumed. Marginal universe widening (the frozen alternates list) or a
modestly longer history is a plausible path to clearing the floor honestly — no need to
jump to denser bars yet. That decision is the user's fork, unchanged in kind but improved
in odds.
