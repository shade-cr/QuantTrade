# B0017 — phase5_earnings_premium: PRE-REGISTRATION (frozen before any data look)

**Committed 2026-07-04, BEFORE the EDGAR fetch results were examined and before any
event-window return was computed on the panel.** Every parameter below is frozen; if the
audit underperforms, none may be revisited without logging a new DSR trial. This is the

dead in large caps; Martineau 2022).

## Evidence base (literature priors, not measured on our panel)

Savor-Wilson, Frazzini-Lamont, "Disappearing EAP" (Rotman): the earnings announcement
premium survives post-2004 specifically in the largest ~500 US names, ~0.30%/event
value-weighted. Mechanism: compensation for bearing announcement risk — holders demand a
premium for carrying the position through the disclosure event.

## Primary rule (frozen)

- Module: `pipeline/primaries_phase5/phase5_earnings_premium.py`
- Signal: **+1 (long) on the first bar whose date falls within
  `[expected_date - entry_days_before, expected_date - 1]`** business days, where
  `expected_date` comes from `pipeline.earnings_events.expected_announcement_schedule`
  (PIT rule: median of trailing 8 gaps, fallback 91d, generated only from PAST 8-K
  acceptance timestamps). One signal per expected event (no re-fire inside the window).
  0 otherwise. **No short leg** (the premium is a long risk-premium claim).
- `entry_days_before = 3` (business days). Frozen. Rationale: the premium accrues in the
  immediate pre-announcement window; 3 BD tolerates the scheduler's few-day error without
  diluting the event into unconditional exposure.
- The triple barrier (h10, tp 1.5 / sl 1.5 ATR — the pinned pooled-audit geometry) manages
  the exit; the announcement itself lands inside the 10-bar window by construction.
- Scheduler knowledge discipline: the expectation for event k is generated by announcement
  k-1 (`known_asof` stamp); a signal may only fire on bars AFTER `known_asof`.

## Meta-features (added for ALL primaries, not just this one)

- `days_to_expected_earnings` (capped at 63), `days_since_last_announcement` (capped 63) —
  computed from the same PIT schedule/cache. NaN before a ticker's first cached announcement.

## Falsification criteria (frozen)

- `median_active_fold_sharpe_min`: 0.5
- `n_trades_total_min`: 100 (43 names × ~80 events × meta pass-through leaves headroom;
  if the meta filters below 100 the verdict is capacity-discard, not survival)
- Breadth: ≥ 5 assets with ≥ 30 trades and positive aggregate best-model Sharpe — the
  premium is a panel-wide claim; single-name survival is not support.
- DSR: this is trial ≥1 of the earnings family; day-aggregate counting per
